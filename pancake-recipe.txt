Ingredients:
 - 1/2 bottle of beer
 - 1/2 cup of milk
 - 3 eggs
 - 5 tablespoons of flour
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - sip of olive oil

Directions:
 1. Put eggs, beer, flour, milk, salt, oul into a big bowl in that order. Stir til no lumps are left.

 2. Preheat a pan.

 3. Fry a small peace of butter and spread a big spoon of dough in the pan. Flip when hard on one side.
 When the pancake is golden on both sides, put on an extra plate.

 4. Repeat until no dough is left.
